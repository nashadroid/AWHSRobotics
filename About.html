About BEST Robotics

BEST stands for "Boosting Engineering, Science, and Technology" and is a national robotics competition. Every fall, BEST releases a fun and exciting gameboard that correlates to real-life applications for robots. Teams across the nation then build robots to compete in their local and regional competitions. Students who participate in BEST competitions gain hands-on experience in STEM field careers. About The Team

We are a team of highschool and junior high students from Anthony Wayne High School in Whitehouse, Ohio. We make robots to compete in BEST competitions each year and strive to have superior designs. Our goal is to have great teamwork in order to efficicently create well performing machines. We strive to promote science, technology, engineering, and mathematics throughout our school and community.
